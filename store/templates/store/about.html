Our Background

Who we are & What we do?

The BookSpot is a Rwandan e-commerce network that links book writers, publishers, and retailers with thoughtful customers eager to purchase physical copies of books. Millions of enthusiastic customers and sellers would be connected via this two-sided internet marketplace. It won't just be an internet marketplace; it will also be a community where Rwandan authors' works can be promoted and sold.

Our company will use various communication methods to contact our consumers, particularly book authors, in order to promote and provide good services to our clients.Our market research team will help you maximize earnings by developing increasingly efficient methods of measuring the competition. They will submit their findings every two months.
